Product Description

The Kobe Protro 6 "Red All-Star" is a special edition colorway of Kobe Bryant's sixth signature shoe, the Nike Kobe 6 Protro, released in commemoration of Kobe's participation in the NBA All-Star Game. This particular colorway features a predominantly red upper with contrasting accents and details inspired by the All-Star Game's aesthetic. The Kobe 6 Protro silhouette is known for its lightweight construction, responsive cushioning, and support, making it a favorite among basketball players and collectors alike. The "Protro" designation indicates that the shoe has been retrofitted with modern performance upgrades while retaining the original design elements.
