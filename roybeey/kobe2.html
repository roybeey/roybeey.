Product Description

The "Reverse Grinch" is another special edition colorway of the Nike Kobe 6 Protro, inspired by the original "Grinch" colorway but with a twist. Instead of the vibrant green upper of the classic "Grinch," the Reverse Grinch features a predominantly black upper with green accents, giving it a darker and more subdued appearance. This colorway offers a fresh take on the iconic design while still paying homage to the original "Grinch" theme. Like other Kobe 6 Protro models, the Reverse Grinch retains the performance enhancements of the original shoe while updating materials and technology for modern basketball play.
