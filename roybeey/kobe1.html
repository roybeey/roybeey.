Product Description

The Kobe Protro 6 "Grinch" is a special edition colorway of Kobe Bryant's sixth signature shoe, the Nike Kobe 6. The "Grinch" nickname comes from its vibrant green color scheme, reminiscent of the Dr. Seuss character, the Grinch. These shoes were originally released in 2010 and quickly became one of the most sought-after Kobe colorways due to their unique look and association with the holiday season. The "Protro" designation indicates that the shoe has been retrofitted with modern performance upgrades while maintaining the original design aesthetic.
